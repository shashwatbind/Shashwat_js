ClassZee – Your Gateway to Merchant Navy, STCW Certification & CBSE Success

ClassZee is India's most trusted free platform for Merchant Navy aspirants, seafarers needing STCW certification, and CBSE Board students. We provide high-quality, easy-to-understand study materials, mock tests, and career guidance — all at zero cost.

🚢 Merchant Navy Preparation (GP Rating, MEK & GSK)

Cracking the GP Rating entrance or MEK (Marine Engineering Knowledge) exam requires focused preparation. ClassZee offers chapter-wise MCQs, detailed notes on ship terminology, engineering basics, watchkeeping, and navigation. Our GSK (General Ship Knowledge) modules cover cargo handling, maritime law, and safety protocols — essential for your pre-sea training.

⚓ STCW Course – Mandatory Safety Training for Seafarers

The STCW (Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping) basic safety training is mandatory for every professional going to sea. It includes four key modules: Personal Survival Techniques (PST), Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting (FPFF), Elementary First Aid (EFA), and Personal Safety & Social Responsibility (PSSR). ClassZee provides dedicated study guides, quiz banks, and scenario-based questions to help you pass the STCW assessments with confidence.

📚 CBSE Board – Strong Academic Foundation

We haven't forgotten school students. Our CBSE Board section includes notes, sample papers, and revision guides for classes 9–12, especially in Science and Mathematics — subjects that form the backbone of marine engineering and navigation careers.
